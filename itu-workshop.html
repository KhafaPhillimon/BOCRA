ITU Capacity Building

Enhancing Botswana's capacity in producing national statistics on Telecommunications and ICT Indicators.

28 - 30 May 2018
Gaborone, Botswana
ITU & BOCRA Partnership

Workshop Overview

The International Telecommunication Union (ITU), in collaboration with the Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority (BOCRA), organized a specialized capacity building workshop focused on ICT Indicators. This workshop aimed to strengthen the national capacity for collecting, processing, and disseminating information and communication technology (ICT) statistics.

Key Objectives

  • Understanding international standards for telecommunication and ICT indicators.
  • Improving data collection methodologies for household and business ICT usage.
  • Aligning national measurement frameworks with ITU's World Telecommunication/ICT Indicators (WTI).
  • Facilitating knowledge exchange between regional regulatory bodies and statistical offices.

The workshop brought together representatives from various government ministries, regulatory agencies, and the national statistical office to ensure a harmonized approach to measuring digital development in Botswana.

Information & Resources

For more information on ITU capacity development or to access the latest ICT indicator reports, please visit the ITU Academy or contact our information desk.
