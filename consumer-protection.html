Know Your Rights

Consumer Rights & Responsibilities

Your Rights

  • To receive clear information about services and tariffs.
  • To be protected from misleading or deceptive conduct.
  • To privacy and protection of personal data.
  • To timely and efficient dispute resolution.
  • To switch service providers without undue barriers.

Your Responsibilities

  • To pay for services consumed within agreed timelines.
  • To use communication services legally and ethically.
  • To protect your devices and account passwords.
  • To report faults or billing discrepancies immediately.
  • To follow the service provider's internal complaint process first.
Stay Informed

Consumer Advice & Tips

Protect Against Scams

Be wary of unsolicited calls or messages asking for personal information or bank details. BOCRA and your provider will NEVER ask for your PIN via SMS.

Monitor Your Usage

Check your data consumption regularly. Heavy background data usage on smartphones can lead to "missing airtime" complaints.

Understand Tariffs

Compare plans across providers. BOCRA publishes tariff comparisons periodically to help you make informed choices.
