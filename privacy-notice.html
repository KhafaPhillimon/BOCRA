Privacy Notice

BOCRA is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data in accordance with the **Botswana Data Protection Act, 2024 (BDPA)**.

What We Collect

We collect personal data necessary for our regulatory functions, including names, contact details of licensees, suppliers, and applicants. Security data is also collected at our premises.

Your Rights

Under the BDPA, you have the following rights:

  • **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • **Deletion**: Request deletion of data no longer needed.
  • **Withdrawal**: Withdraw consent at any time.

For inquiries regarding your data, contact our Data Protection Officer at info@bocra.org.bw.
