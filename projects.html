Key Strategic Projects

Nationwide Access

Universal Access & Service Fund (UASF)

BOCRA oversees the implementation of connectivity projects under the UASF to bridge the digital divide.

National Broadband Strategy

Coordinated approach to building a robust ICT ecosystem and high-speed networks across Botswana.

Broadcasting

Digital Switchover (DSO)

Efficiency

Infrastructure Sharing

Framework requiring operators to share passive infrastructure to reduce environment impact and costs.

Periodic reporting on cost modelling to ensures fair pricing and sustainable regulation.
