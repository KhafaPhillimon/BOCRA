Home Complaints

Lodge a Complaint

BOCRA helps consumers resolve disputes with service providers after all internal remedies have been exhausted.

01

Contact Provider

Lodge your complaint directly with the service provider (Mascom, Orange, BTC, etc.) and get a reference number.

02

Wait for Resolution

Allow the provider a maximum of 14 working days to address your issue. Obtain a written response if possible.

03

Lodge with BOCRA

If the issue is unresolved or you are unsatisfied with the provider's response, lodge your complaint with us.

Before You Begin

Ensure you have the following information and documents ready to expedite your complaint.

Reference number from Provider
ID / Passport Copy
Billing Statement (if applicable)
Copies of correspondence

Complaint Filing Form

Please provide detailed information to help us investigate your case.
