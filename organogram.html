Mission in Action

Organisational Objectives

The strategic objectives that guide BOCRA's work across all departments.

1

Facilitating Market Entry

Facilitating entry of new service providers into the market to promote competition and innovation in Botswana's communications sector.

2

Efficient Spectrum Management

Managing the frequency spectrum efficiently in order to maximize frequency availability for all communications services.

3

Universal Service Financing

Making provision for the financing of universal service in areas where this would otherwise be uneconomic and for special tariffs for disadvantaged users.

4

Consumer Protection

Promoting the development of user representation and instituting consumer protection measures to safeguard the public interest.

5

Organisational Excellence

Developing BOCRA as a dynamic, open, transparent and respected organisation with highly competent and well-motivated staff — a model regulatory body.

Departments

Our Five Departments

Each department is headed by a Director and plays a vital role in BOCRA's regulatory mission.

Compliance & Monitoring

Responsible for ensuring adherence to regulations and monitoring the performance of licensed operators across all sectors of the communications industry.

Corporate Support

Provides administrative, financial, human resources, and ICT support services that enable BOCRA to operate efficiently and effectively.

Business Development

Focuses on market analysis, licensing, policy development, and stakeholder engagement to drive sustainable growth in Botswana's communications sector.

Technical Services

Manages spectrum allocation, type approval, technical standards, and the deployment of technical infrastructure including the Spectrum Monitoring House.

Corporate Communications & Relations

Manages BOCRA's public image, media relations, stakeholder communication, and consumer education to ensure transparency and public trust.
