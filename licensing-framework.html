ICT Licensing Framework

The converged licensing framework designed to foster competition, innovation, and high-quality services in Botswana's ICT sector.

Overview of the Framework

BOCRA implemented the new and converged ICT licensing framework in September 2015. The framework was meant to create a more conducive environment for ICT development, meeting the demand for real-time high quality and affordable services, and accommodating emerging players for increased competitiveness.

Network Facilities Provider (NFP)

This licence category covers entities that own and operate communications infrastructure and facilities used for providing services.

Application Requirements

Services and Applications Provider (SAP)

This category is for entities that provide communications services and applications to end-users using the facilities of NFPs.
