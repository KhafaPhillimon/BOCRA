COVID-19 Information

Disseminating critical info and ensuring service continuity.

Public Health

TOGETHER WE CAN BEAT COVID-19

BOCRA remains committed to leveraging ICTs to support Botswana's resilience and recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic.

During the pandemic, we accelerated the adoption of e-licensing and e-payment systems to ensure service continuity while protecting public health. We continue to promote digital tools for remote work, education, and health information dissemination.

Critical Actions:

  • Promotion of 0800 toll-free lines for health emergencies.
  • License fee holidays for critical broadcasting sectors.
  • Prioritization of health-related network traffic.

Stay Safe

Use digital channels where possible. Together, we are stronger.
