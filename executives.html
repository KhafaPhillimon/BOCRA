Executive Management

The leadership team driving BOCRA's vision for a connected and digitally driven Botswana.

Our Leadership Team

BOCRA is managed by a team of dedicated professionals with extensive experience in economic regulation, technical services, corporate governance, and digital transformation.

Chief Executive

Mr. Martin Mokgware

Mr. Mokgware has served as the Chief Executive of BOCRA since December 2017. An economist by profession, he previously held senior management roles within BOCRA, including Director for Broadband and Universal Services, and Director for Market Development, focusing on tariff setting and cost model development.

He has chaired the Universal Access and Service Fund (UASF) of Botswana since September 2018, which aims to provide connectivity to rural populations. Mr. Mokgware also led the development of Botswana's National Broadband Strategy.

Director Business Development

Mr. Murphy Setshwane

Mr. Setshwane's academic background includes an MSc in Policy Economics from the University of Illinois, USA, a Post Graduate Diploma in Telecommunications Regulation from the University of Westminster, UK, and a BA in Economics.

He has also served as the Director of Licensing and Postal Regulation and as Acting Chief Executive Officer in October 2025.

Director Corporate Services

Mr. Peter Tladinyane

Mr. Tladinyane holds an MA in Management from the University of the Witwatersrand, South Africa. He also earned a BA in Economics & Statistics and a Diploma in Statistics from the University of Botswana, along with a Certificate in Human Resources Management from the University of Pretoria, South Africa.

Director Finance

Ms. Bonny Mine

Ms. Mine holds an MBA from Henley Business School, University of Reading, UK. She is a Chartered Institute of Management Accountant (CIMA) and has a BCom in Accounting from the University of Botswana.

Director Technical Services

Mr. Bathopi Luke

A professional engineer, Mr. Luke has played a significant role in developing Botswana's broadcasting sector as the former Director of Broadcasting Regulation. His work included promoting competition and overseeing the liberalization of broadcasting platforms.

He also served as a Telecommunications Compliance Director, monitoring the Quality of Service and Experience for mobile and fixed networks. Mr. Luke possesses extensive experience as a Telecommunications Engineer, with expertise in spectrum planning, assignment, and monitoring.

Director of Licensing

Ms. Tebogo Mmoshe

Appointed Director of Licensing in 2021, Ms. Mmoshe is responsible for facilitating licensing across BOCRA's regulated sectors, including postal, broadcasting, internet, and ICT. She brings 12 years of experience in communications regulation at BOCRA and 18 years in Accounting and Auditing in Botswana.

Ms. Mmoshe became a Chartered Accountant in 2006 and obtained an MBA from the University of Derby in 2013. She is also a Fellow Member of the Botswana Institute of Chartered Accountants (BICA).

Director Broadband and Universal Service

Ms. Maitseo Ratladi

Ms. Ratladi's qualifications include an MBA and a BBA Honors from the University of Stellenbosch, South Africa. She also holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Telecommunications Regulation from the University of Westminster, UK, an Associate Diploma in Banking from the Institute of Bankers, Botswana, and a BA in Economics and Accounting from the University of Botswana.

Director Legal, Compliance & Board Secretary

Ms. Joyce Isa-Molwane

Ms. Isa-Molwane is an attorney admitted to practice in Botswana, with 25 years of experience. She joined BOCRA in December 2017.

Her responsibilities encompass corporate governance oversight, ensuring compliance of regulated entities, and providing legal advice to the BOCRA and Universal Access and Service Fund (UASF) Boards. She is involved in implementing Botswana's National Cybersecurity Strategy and developing cybersecurity legislation. Ms. Isa-Molwane holds an LLB from the University of Botswana.
