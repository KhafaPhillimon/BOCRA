Guidelines

Procurement Standards

BOCRA ensures transparency and fairness in all its procurement processes.

Tender Submission

Tenders must be submitted in the format specified in the tender document. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.

Supplier Registration

Vendors wishing to do business with BOCRA must be registered on our Supplier Database. Applications are reviewed quarterly.

Adjudication

All bids are evaluated by the Tender Committee based on technical compliance, financial proposal, and PPADB requirements.
