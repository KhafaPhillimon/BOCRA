Tariffs & Pricing

Ensuring fair and affordable communication services.

Costing & Pricing

Communications Tariffs

BOCRA ensures that communication services in Botswana remain affordable and competitive through active regulation of tariffs and termination rates.

Termination Rate Reductions

  • Current: P0.13 per minute
  • July 2025: P0.09 per minute
  • July 2027 Target: P0.02 per minute

Retail Tariff Ceilings

BOCRA publishes ceiling retail tariffs for BTC, Mascom, and Orange. These serve as maximum prices, encouraging competition through bundles and promotional offers.

Regional Roaming (SADC ONA)

BOCRA is part of the SADC One Network Area (ONA) framework, collaborating with Malawi, Lesotho, Mozambique, Namibia, Zambia, and Zimbabwe to harmonize and reduce roaming tariffs within the region.
